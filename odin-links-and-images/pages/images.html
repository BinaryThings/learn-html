This page covers common image formats, but you may also be looking for the main page and the about page.

JPGs

JPG images are good for photos, with large color pallets and gradients.

GIFs

GIFs are good for animations. Color pallet is limited and only binary transparency is supported.

PNGs

PNGs are good for diagrams and icons. Full transparency support and no color pallet limitations. Larger file sizes for photos compared to JPG.

SVGs

SVGs are amazing. They use vectors which avoid pixelation. Use them wherever you can. Not good for images with text.
