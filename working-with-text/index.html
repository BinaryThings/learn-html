This is my first webpage with readable content. In this page, I will be going over the basics of inputting and organizing text.

Text spacing will be compressed to no longer than a single space. Paragraph tags will be needed for separation otherwise all text will be contained to a single large paragraph.

The use of bold and italics are not as simple as expected, as they indicate importance or emphasis beyond the visual usage. This is important for screen readers for the visually impaired. Headers are similarly more than meets the eye, and dictate actual importance in a hierarchical way.
